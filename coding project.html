Introduction To Solar Cars

What is a solar car?

A solar car is a vehicle that is powered by electricity through the use of solar energy. Solar cars usually run on only power from the sun, although some models will supplement that power using a battery, or use solar panels to recharge batteries or run auxiliary systems for a car that mainly uses battery power.

What is the principle of the solar car?

The basic principle of a solar car is depending on a solar array that uses photovoltaic cells (PV cells) to convert sunlight into electricity. The car uses the energy stored in a battery during and after charging it from a solar panel.
